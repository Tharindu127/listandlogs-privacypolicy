2. What List & Logs Does

List & Logs is a smart shopping tracker that helps you plan, track, and analyze your purchases by distinguishing between required and non-required spending in real-time. The App allows you to:

  • Create and manage shopping lists
  • Log actual spending during checkout
  • Track your expenses and identify savings opportunities
  • Visualize spending patterns through detailed analytics
  • Back up your data to your personal cloud storage (Google Drive or iCloud)

By turning everyday shopping into measurable insights, List & Logs empowers you to spend mindfully and save more over time.

3.1 Guest Mode

List & Logs offers a Guest Mode that allows you to use the app without creating an account. Here's what you need to know about Guest Mode:

3.1.1 What Guest Mode Offers

As a guest user, you can:

  • ✓ Create and manage unlimited shopping lists
  • ✓ Track your spending and finalize purchases
  • ✓ View reports and analytics
  • ✓ Create saved list templates and item library
  • ✓ Set local reminders for your lists
  • ✓ Customize app settings (theme, currency, notifications)

3.1.2 Guest Mode Limitations

Guest users cannot:

  • Back up data to cloud storage (Google Drive/iCloud)
  • Sync data across multiple devices
  • Upload a profile picture
  • Receive server-side push notifications

3.1.3 Guest Data Storage

Important: All guest data is stored locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Upload guest data to our servers
  • Store guest data in Firebase or any cloud service
  • Collect personal information from guest users
  • Track guest users across devices

Data Persistence: Your guest data will remain on your device until you uninstall the app or explicitly exit guest mode and choose to delete your data. If you uninstall the app, all guest data will be permanently lost.

3.1.4 Converting from Guest to Registered User

You can convert from guest mode to a registered account at any time:

  1. Go to Settings → Account → "Sign In to Save Data"
  2. Create a new account or sign in with an existing account
  3. Your guest data will be migrated to your new account
  4. You'll gain access to cloud backup and all registered user features

3.1.5 Guest Data Collection

For guest users, we collect minimal technical data only:

  • Device model and OS version (for app compatibility)
  • App version (for bug fixes)
  • Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics, anonymized)
  • Advertising data (via Google AdMob, see Section 7)

We do NOT collect: Names, emails, passwords, or any personal identifiers from guest users.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Regular Security Audits: We monitor for vulnerabilities and security threats.

However, no system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Use List & Logs at your own risk.
